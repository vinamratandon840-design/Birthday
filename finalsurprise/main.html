My dearest Audry,

I don't think I could ever put into words how much you mean to me, but today felt like the right day to try — so I sat down and wrote you the longest letter I've ever written, because you deserve every line of it.

Your laugh, the way you light up a room without even trying, the way you care for everyone around you before you think of yourself — it's all the reason I fell for you, again and again, on days you didn't even know you were being wonderful.

I still remember the small things more than the big ones. The way you get excited over the tiniest good news. The way you say the same joke slightly differently every time and somehow it's funnier each time. The way you show up for the people you love without ever being asked to. I notice all of it, even when I don't say it out loud.

Every memory we've made together, even the small, silly, forgettable-to-anyone-else ones, I hold onto tightly. The random conversations that turned into hours. Forever yours,
